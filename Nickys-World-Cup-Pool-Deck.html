The Players

Six managers, six teams each

Nicky Gary Gabey Diccy Lee Reilly

Each manager drafts six national teams before kickoff. 48 teams, 12 groups of four; the top two of each group plus the eight best third-placed teams reach the Round of 32. Your score is the sum of points your six teams earn all tournament long.

How It Works

The fine print

  • •Each of your six teams scores on its own — add them up for your running total.
  • •Group placement bonuses are awarded once each group concludes.
  • •Knockout matches decided on penalties count as a win for the side that advances.
  • •The "all teams advance" bonus (+3) needs all six of your teams to reach the Round of 32.
The Details

Standings & daily briefing

Standings & Tiebreakers

Ranked by total points. Ties are broken at the commissioner's discretion (suggested: whose teams reached the furthest stage). Nicky is commissioner.

Daily Briefing

Every morning, all managers get a recap: yesterday's results and points, the updated standings, and the day's fixtures with each team's owner — plus a live tracker kept current all day.
